# AI Fashion & Product Campaign Studio

### **Advanced Generative Workspace for High-End Fashion Branding**

A comprehensive, professional-grade fashion studio and campaign builder powered by Google Gemini. This workspace enables creators to design, retouch, and build full-scale ad campaigns through a unified multimodal interface, bridging the gap between raw product photography and market-ready visual assets.

---

## üöÄ Key Features

*   **Retouching Lab:** Integrated canvas-based masking and drawing tools (Brush, Eraser) for precision AI-driven image editing and targeted in-painting.
*   **Ad Campaign Builder:** Dynamic design layer for adding headlines and subtext overlays, transforming generated imagery into professional marketing collateral.
*   **Generative Studio Engine:** High-fidelity multimodal generation for products and fashion models, featuring advanced control over lighting, style, and environment.
*   **Component-Based Workspace:** Specialized workflows for uploading custom products, models, and backgrounds to maintain consistent brand identity.
*   **Multi-Angle Synthesis:** Orchestrate complex visual transformations while preserving product details across various aspect ratios and compositions.
