# ArchiDesigner AI

### **World-Class AI Studio for Architectural Visualizations**

A sophisticated AI Architect Studio designed to transform standard property photography into high-end architectural visualizations and professional 3D perspectives. Leveraging Google Gemini's multimodal capabilities, ArchiDesigner allows architects, real estate developers, and designers to reimagine spaces through a seamless blend of visual analysis, semantic grounding, and generative precision.

---

## üöÄ Key Features

*   **Architectural Transformation:** Converts raw house photos and site images into polished professional-grade visualizations and 3D perspectives.
*   **Object-Level Retouching:** Advanced object detection and segmentation (masks/bounding boxes) for targeted editing of specific architectural elements like windows, facades, or landscaping.
*   **Intelligent Grounding:** Integrated web grounding to inform design decisions with real-world trends, material specifications, and architectural context.
*   **Multimodal Chat Workflow:** A conversational interface that handles multiple image uploads, providing AI-driven suggestions and iterative refinement for every visualization.
*   **Project Album Management:** Comprehensive session tracking with persistent albums, gallery history, and stateful visualization versions.
